A Brief History of Hogwarts

Founded in the 10th century by Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff, and Salazar Slytherin in the Scottish Highlands due to persecution by Muggles, the school gathered students from all over the world. Many charms and spells hide Hogwarts from the Muggles, only letting them see a ruined castle. Much of the design and construction of Hogwarts remains an enigma even today. Even the headmasters themselves do not know all the secrets of Hogwarts...

The most recent major historical event to occur at Hogwarts was the great battle between the forces of Voldemort and Harry Potter and his allies. That was the end of Voldemort's reign of terror in the wizarding world. Many heroes were made that day and many heroes were lost.
